WWII USN Fighter Image Classifier

Upload a picture of a USN fighter from WWII and this app will tell you which one it is. Photos of actual aircraft work well, but so do "good" paintings and pictures of model aircraft with sufficient realism. For example a skilled hobbyist model aircraft should work, but a LEGO aircraft probably won't.

CLASSES:

TBF Avenger, F8F Bearcat, F2A Buffalo, F4U Corsair, SBD Dauntless, TBD Devastator, F6F Hellcat, SB2C Helldiver, F4F Wildcat
